03 : About me

My name is Trejon Stallsworth

Hi there! I'm Trejon Stallsworth from Denver, Colorado. I attended the Full-Stack Software Engineering program at Flatiron School. I've been programming for a little over a year and a half. I have experience in JavaScript, Ruby and Python. I also have a lot of practice with frameworks and libraries including Rails, Sinatra, React, Redux and more. In my spare time I enjoy digging into machine learning with Python and experimenting with IoT, especially focusing on the Raspberry Pi.

I completed two years of coursework in finance at Metropolitan State University of Denver. While attending the university and working full time as a bank teller, my work was drastically changed by new technology. A new banking system rolled out, changing my daily responsibilities as well as the entire customer experience. The impact of the new system and efficiency it provided, rekindled my curiosity and passion for the power of technology.

I spent the next year taking online courses to learn more about different technologies and trying to better understand the industry, ultimately leading me to Flatiron School. Aside from my core curriculum, I developed a deeper passion for problem solving and programmatic thinking through various data structure and algorithm questions. Through the program and many projects I've built, my passion quickly turned into an obsession. I'm excited to continue expanding my knowledge and developing my skill set in the dynamic world of tech.
